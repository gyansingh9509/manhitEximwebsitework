Our Courses

We offers job-focused training programs that build real-world skills for employment. Our expert-designed courses in Export-Import, Logistics, Retail, Office Management, Digital Marketing, and Soft Skills combine hands-on learning with placement support to make you job-ready. With a strong track record of successful placements, we are your trusted partner in skill development and career growth.

Our Courses

Apparel Made-ups and Home Furnishing

This course covers garment manufacturing, textile design, and home furnishing, focusing on practical skills, sustainability, and industry trends for careers in production and design.

Beauty and Wellness

This course covers grooming, skincare, haircare, makeup, and wellness, preparing students with essential techniques for careers in salons, spas, and the beauty industry.

Construction

This construction course offers practical training in building techniques, safety, and project management, covering masonry, carpentry, plumbing, and electrical work for careers in infrastructure and real estate.

Sports

This program covers sports management, coaching, fitness, nutrition, and event planning, preparing students for careers in sports academies, fitness centers, and event management companies.

Handicraft & Carpet

This course explores Indian handicrafts and carpet weaving, teaching traditional and modern techniques. It builds artistic and entrepreneurial skills to preserve and promote indigenous crafts.

Retail

This retail course covers customer service, sales, inventory, merchandising, and operations, with practical training through simulations and internships for careers in supermarkets, malls, and retail chains.

Banking and Accounting

This course covers banking operations, accounting, taxation, auditing, and digital banking, training students in software and financial analysis for roles in banks, finance, and corporate sectors.

Healthcare

This healthcare course covers patient care, medical terminology, first aid, safety, and basic nursing, preparing students for roles in hospitals, clinics, and community health centers.

Electrical

This program covers electrical installation, maintenance, wiring, circuit design, and safety, training students to become electricians, maintenance technicians, or supervisors in the electrical industry.

Electronic

This electronics course covers circuit assembly, testing, repair, and device usage, preparing graduates for careers in electronics manufacturing, servicing, and quality control.

Green Jobs

This green jobs course covers renewable energy, waste management, water conservation, and eco-friendly practices, preparing students for careers in sustainability and the expanding green economy.

Tourism & Hospitality

This course covers travel management, hotel operations, customer service, and event planning, with practical training to prepare students for careers in tourism, hospitality, and hotel management.
