WELCOME TO
Manhit Exim PVT. LTD.
(Innovate Educate Elevate)

Manhit Exim Pvt. Ltd. is committed to contributing to the advancement of private skill-based and vocational education in India. Through a holistic approach, we aim to uplift the overall quality of skill development in the private sector. As industry standards in vocational training continue to rise over the coming years, Manhit Exim Pvt. Ltd. seeks to collaborate with social welfare organizations and educational institutions to promote and enhance private skill-based education and training across the country.

This may be a small step toward participating in Skillful India, but with meaningful effort, sincerity, and transparent thinking, we are committed to turning it into a significant milestone.

Our Courses

we offer practical, job-oriented training programs designed to build real-world skills that lead to employment. Our expert-crafted courses in Export-Import, Logistics, Retail, Office Management, Digital Marketing, and Soft Skills focus on hands-on learning and industry relevance. Each program is designed to make you job-ready through practical sessions, real case studies, and placement support. Whether you're starting your career or upskilling for better opportunities, our training ensures you gain the technical and personal skills needed to succeed in today’s competitive job market. With a strong track record of successful placements and professional growth, Manhit Exim Pvt. Ltd. is your trusted partner for skill development and career advancement.

Placement (Rozgar Mela)

we organize Rozgar Melas to directly connect job-ready candidates with leading employers through on-the-spot interviews and hiring opportunities. These employment fairs bridge the gap between skilled talent and industry needs, making the hiring process faster and more effective. With resume support, interview preparation, and access to real job openings, our Rozgar Melas serve as powerful platforms for immediate employment and career growth.
