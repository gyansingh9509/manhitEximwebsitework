About the Company

Manhit Exim Pvt. Ltd. (MEPL) was established in 2018 by Mr. Vikas Nagar, who serves as the Director of the organization. Since its inception, MEPL has been committed to providing high-quality, multi-disciplinary learning management and training delivery solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of corporations, academic institutions, and individual learners. With a strong vision to bridge skill gaps and enhance workforce readiness, MEPL has successfully extended its reach across 15 to 18 states in India. The organization’s services are designed to be flexible, scalable, and aligned with industry standards, thereby enabling clients to stay competitive in an evolving marketplace. Over the years, MEPL has built a solid reputation for delivering impactful training programs, leveraging modern pedagogical methods and advanced technology platforms. The company’s expertise spans across various sectors and disciplines, ensuring comprehensive skill development and continuous learning opportunities for its clients. Driven by a passion for excellence and innovation, MEPL continues to expand its footprint and evolve its offerings, contributing to the growth and development of human capital nationwide.

Our Vision

MEPL is imparting education and works to create and sustain an environment conducive to the development of India, partnering industry, government, and civil society, through advisory and consultative processes.

Our Mission

We are running the skill development courses under the various schemes of different ministries such as UPSDM, NSDC , SSC, RSLDC, ASDM, NULM We work on skills, trained and assist our students in finding suitable job opportunities for a better future.

MEPL have award winning team of professional certified Trainers, Graphic Designers, IT Experts who have 5+years experience in IT Industry. Since 5+ years success, conducting various government training and skill development programs. We are authorized training partner with NATIONAL SKILL DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION (NSDC).
